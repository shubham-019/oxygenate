Broadleaf Lady Palm

    • Rhapis excelsa is popularly known as Broad Lady Palm or Broadleaf Lady Palm. It is an excellent choice for an air purifying plant. It can break down various toxic air pollutants such as nitrogen oxide, toluene, benzene, xylene, and formaldehyde in indoor air. Since they can grow up to six feet, they are ideal for corners of any room.
