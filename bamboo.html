Bamboo

    • A bamboo plant is a great outdoor plant for homes that may not get a lot of sunlight. Although this plant will grow taller with more sunlight, it can still thrive in low-light conditions. Mature bamboo plants will grow from 4-12 feet tall with a span of 3-5 feet. Provide ample space in your flowerpot for their growth.
    Bamboo palms also need high-quality potting soil to thrive. Similar to the areca plant, bamboo plants also need ideal drainage to thrive.
