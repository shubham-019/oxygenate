Areca Palm

    • Golden palms, butterfly palms, and yellow palms are all names for Areca palms. These lovely areca palms are tropical trees that produce a lot of oxygen, and having two huge plants in close proximity to each other will significantly enhance the amount of oxygen in your home.
    They not only produce high levels of oxygen, but they also remove dangerous pollutants such as formaldehyde, xylene, benzene, and toluene from the air.
